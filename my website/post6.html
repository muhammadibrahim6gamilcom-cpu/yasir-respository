How to Earn Money with AdSense

Google AdSense allows website owners to earn money by showing ads. Once your site is approved, ads are displayed automatically, and you earn when visitors click or view ads.

Requirements

  • Minimum 15–20 original articles
  • About, Contact, Privacy pages
  • Mobile-friendly design
  • No copyright content

Tips for Approval

  • Write informative, original content
  • Update website regularly
  • Use clear navigation and footer
  • Wait 2–4 weeks for new websites before applying

Ad Placement

Use Google AdSense dashboard to get ad codes and place them in your HTML where you want ads to appear. Avoid placing too many ads; 2–3 per page is safe.

Once approved, monitor your performance, experiment with ad types, and continue creating quality content to maximize earnings.
