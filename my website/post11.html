How to Start a Blog

Blogging is a great way to share your knowledge, experiences, and earn online. Starting a blog requires planning, content creation, and promotion.

Steps to Start a Blog

  1. Choose your niche (topic you love)
  2. Create your website using HTML, CSS, and optional CMS like WordPress
  3. Write original articles (minimum 800 words)
  4. Add About, Contact, Privacy pages
  5. Host your website online
  6. Apply for Google AdSense to earn money

Content Tips

  • Focus on quality over quantity
  • Make articles helpful and informative
  • Update website regularly
  • Use images and code examples to make articles engaging

Conclusion

Blogging helps you build authority, attract visitors, and earn through AdSense. Consistency and originality are key to successful blogging.
