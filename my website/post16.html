How to Add Google AdSense to Your Website

Google AdSense is the easiest way to earn money online by showing ads on your website. After approval, AdSense will provide code that you can place in your HTML pages.

Steps to Add AdSense

  1. Create a Google AdSense account
  2. Add your website and wait for approval
  3. Once approved, get the ad code from AdSense dashboard
  4. Paste the code in HTML where you want the ad to appear

Example Placement

Place ads in blog posts, sidebar, or header/footer:

Tips for AdSense Approval

  • Website must have 15–20 original articles
  • About, Contact, Privacy pages
  • Mobile-friendly design
  • High-quality and helpful content

Conclusion

Adding AdSense correctly helps you earn money from your website traffic. Focus on quality content and user experience to maximize revenue.
