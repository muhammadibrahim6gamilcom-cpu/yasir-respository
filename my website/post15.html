Introduction to SEO for Beginners

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) improves your website's visibility on Google and other search engines. Learning SEO is essential for bloggers and web developers.

On-Page SEO

  • Use relevant keywords in headings and paragraphs
  • Write original, helpful content
  • Add meta title and description
  • Optimize images with alt tags

Off-Page SEO

  • Build backlinks from other websites
  • Share your articles on social media
  • Encourage reviews and mentions

Technical SEO

  • Ensure fast page loading
  • Mobile-friendly design
  • Proper URL structure
  • Secure HTTPS connection

SEO increases traffic, which is crucial for AdSense and earning online. Regular updates and quality content improve search rankings.

Conclusion

Understanding SEO basics helps your website grow organically. Combine it with quality content, responsive design, and AdSense readiness.
